Castle Quest - Text adventure game

Welcome, adventurer, to our castle! A new and exciting journey awaits. My name is Josser, the queen's jester. I will join on your quest.In each castle room, you will face different challenges. You will score points when you overcome these challenges!!! The aim of your quest is to score over 600 points in order to win the game. Your quest consists in:
  • Answering the question that the castellans are asking you. They are not your friends...
  • Choosing one of the two pictures from the right side of the screen to win or lose points.
  • Collecting one of the objects in the room by typing its name and submit your choice.
